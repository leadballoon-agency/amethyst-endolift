## Technical Specifications: Endolift Technology

### How It Works
1. Hair-thin laser fiber (0.2-0.4mm) inserted under skin
2. 1470nm laser energy delivered to subdermal layer
3. Targets water and fat in tissue
4. Immediate skin contraction
5. Long-term collagen stimulation (3-6 months improvement)

### Treatment Process
1. **Consultation:** 30 mins - Assessment and goal setting
2. **Treatment Day:** 1 hour - Local anesthetic + procedure
3. **Recovery:** 1-2 days - Mild swelling, minimal downtime
4. **Results:** 3-6 months - Progressive improvement

### Safety & Credentials
- FDA Approved
- 10+ years use in Europe
- Performed by Advanced Endolift Practitioner
- Local anesthetic for comfort
- Minimal downtime
- Proven safety profile
